## HOMEOWNER RESEARCH: What Homeowners Want (2025 Study)

### Source: Elevate Market Research - 1,200+ Homeowners Surveyed

### THE THREE PILLARS: Trust, Transparency, Quality

---

### PILLAR 1: TRUST - Clear Signals Win Homeowners

#### Clear Pricing is #1 Trust Signal
- **88% say clear pricing builds trust** more than anything else
- **51% say absence of pricing is a dealbreaker**
- Even a starting range or sample estimate makes massive difference
- "Homeowners aren't price shopping, they're CLARITY shopping"

#### Website is First Impression
What turns homeowners OFF:
- 83% - Pushy or aggressive sales tactics = dealbreaker
- 65% - Stock photos or AI-generated images lose trust
- 51% - Can't see what's included in service
- 40% - "Cringy one-liners"

#### Social Media is Now Required
- 1 in 3 Millennials/Gen X say no social media = suspicious
- Looking for: before/after shots, job walkthroughs, crew highlights, real reviews
- Doesn't need to be perfect, needs to be HUMAN and ACTIVE

#### Reviews Must Be Detailed
- Average homeowner reads 5-10 reviews before deciding
- **55% want detailed reviews with before/after photos**
- **45% say scripted/fake reviews kill confidence**
- **44% say professional response to negative review builds trust**

---

### PILLAR 2: TRANSPARENCY - Clear Pricing & Honest Communication

#### Key Stat: 70% would PAY MORE to avoid surprise costs

#### What Homeowners Want:
- Clear, honest pricing (not just "affordable")
- Defined scope of work
- Appointment windows and progress updates
- Warranty terms and service assurances
- Alternative solutions when job allows

#### Follow-Up Rules:
- 53% appreciate TWO follow-ups
- Nearly half say THIRD follow-up is too much
- Send: quote summary, scheduling link, answers to common questions
- Then give space to decide

#### Key Insight: "Aim to inform, not to oversell"
Every touchpoint should answer:
1. What's the process?
2. What's the price?
3. What should I expect next?

---

### PILLAR 3: QUALITY - Speed, Communication, Clarity

#### Speed is Critical
- **87% say fast replies make them more likely to hire**
- **62% will move on if they don't hear back quickly**
- Same-day reply = difference between ghosted or booked

#### What "Quality" Means to Homeowners:
- Fast response times
- Clear communication
- Defined expectations
- Professional follow-through

## VALIDT UX OPTIMIZATION RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Homeowners:
1. **Price Transparency First** - Show quote totals, breakdowns, market comparisons prominently
2. **No Surprises Messaging** - Emphasize "hidden cost detection" and "no surprise" guarantee
3. **Speed Messaging** - "60-second analysis" is major selling point
4. **Trust Indicators** - Real reviews, verified contractors, compliance badges
5. **Clear Process** - Show exactly what happens: Upload → Analyze → Report
6. **Informative Tone** - Educate, don't sell

### For Tradespeople:
1. **Professional Credibility** - License verification, ABN checks build trust
2. **Competitive Positioning** - Show how their quotes compare to market
3. **Efficiency Tools** - Fast, mobile-friendly, works on job site
4. **Business Intelligence** - Help them understand their pricing position

### For Business Owners:
1. **ROI Focus** - Show savings, efficiency gains, time saved
2. **Dashboard Metrics** - Real-time business intelligence
3. **Compliance Assurance** - Reduce legal/regulatory risk
4. **Professional Reports** - Branded PDFs for client communication
