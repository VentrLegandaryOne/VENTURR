## Recommendations for Contractors

### When to Use Enhanced Crew (as tested)
This crew composition is optimal for:
- Roof areas 200-300m² (our test: 150m² is slightly small, but acceptable)
- Projects requiring faster completion (8 days vs. 11 days with standard crew)
- Moderate to complex roofs
- Quality-focused clients willing to pay premium

### Cost Optimization Opportunities
For this specific project, contractors could:
1. **Use Standard Crew** instead of Enhanced:
   - Would take ~11 days instead of 8 days
   - Labor cost would reduce to ~$7,200 (vs. $9,310)
   - Total savings: ~$2,100
   - Better suited for 150m² roof

2. **Consider Apprentice Duo** for budget clients:
   - Would take ~14 days
   - Labor cost would reduce to ~$5,800
   - Total savings: ~$3,500
   - Only for simple, straightforward installations

### Regional Insights
**Sydney Metro pricing (+15%):**
- Highest cost of living adjustment on East Coast
- WorkCover rate 10.81% (vs. 4.5% QLD, 7% VIC)
- Combined effect: ~25% higher labor costs than regional areas
- Contractors should emphasize quality and expertise to justify premium
