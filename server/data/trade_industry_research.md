# Australian Trade Industry Best Practices & SOPs Research

## 1. ELECTRICAL TRADE

### Primary Standards
- **AS/NZS 3000:2018** - Electrical Installations (Wiring Rules)
  - Mandatory compliance from 1 January 2019
  - Covers design, construction, and verification of electrical installations
  - Key requirements:
    - RCD required for all domestic/residential final sub-circuits
    - Additional RCD requirements for non-domestic installations
    - Clearance requirements in switch rooms
    - Cable installation safety requirements
    - Isolator requirements for water heaters, AC, heat pumps
    - Earthing requirements for outbuildings
    - Electric vehicle charging guidelines
    - Arc fault protection requirements

### NSW Advisory Notes (Building Commission NSW)
- Note 3/2025: RCD protection in Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)
- Note 2/2025: RCD compliance requirements
- Note 1/2025: Heat pump hot water installation
- Note 7/2023: Equipotential bonding showers and bathrooms
- Note 6/2023: Solar system layouts
- Note 5/2023: Cable protection through metallic structures
- Note 4/2023: Post-flooding electrical safety inspection
- Note 3/2021: Main switch location and identification
- Note 2/2021: Switches and sockets in wet areas
- Note 1/2018: Common non-compliances in metering installations

### Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS)
- Required for high-risk electrical work
- Must cover: isolation (LOTO), testing, fault finding
- Safe working within 500mm of low-voltage installations
- PPE requirements
- Emergency procedures

### Licensing Requirements
- Certificate of Compliance for Electrical Work (CCEW) mandatory
- From March 2026: All CCEW via BCNSW eCert portal
- Supervision Practice Standard for apprentices

## 2. PLUMBING TRADE

### Primary Standards
- **AS/NZS 3500 Series** - Plumbing and Drainage
  - AS/NZS 3500.1:2025 - Cold water services (design, installation, commissioning)
  - AS/NZS 3500.2 - Sanitary plumbing and drainage
  - AS/NZS 3500.3 - Stormwater drainage
  - AS/NZS 3500.4 - Heated water services
  - AS/NZS 3500.5 - Housing installations

### Plumbing Code of Australia (PCA)
- PCA 2019 and PCA 2022 editions
- Two compliance pathways:
  1. Deemed to Satisfy (DTS) - compliance with AS/NZS 3500
  2. Performance Solution - designed by hydraulic engineer

### NSW Advisory Notes
- Note 1/2025: Heat pump hot water installation
- Note 2/2023: Floor waste and fixture requirements
- Note 1/2023: Drains in other than stable ground
- Note 19/2022: Flexible joints in drains
- Note 18/2022: General accessibility requirements
- Note 21/2021: Cross connection and backflow control
- Note 17/2021: Automatic air elimination valves
- Note 14/2021: Junctions at grade
- Note 13/2021: Tundish installation
- Note 16/2019: Automatic fire sprinkler systems
- Note 13/2018: Overflow relief gully height
- Note 11/2015: Inspection openings
- Note 4/2013: Air Admittance Valves installation
- Note 1/2013: Temperature control devices

### Backflow Prevention Requirements
- Based on risk level of property
- Devices must comply with AS/NZS 3500.1
- Regular testing and certification required
- Licensed plumber with backflow endorsement

### Licensing
- 6-year responsibility period for completed work (WA)
- State-specific licensing authorities
- Inspection requirements vary by region

## 3. ROOFING TRADE

### Primary Standards
- **SA HB 39:2015** - Installation Code for Metal Roof and Wall Cladding
  - Companion to AS 1562.1:2018
  - Minimum roof pitch requirements:
    - Corrugated profiles: minimum 5° (1:12)
    - Standing seam: minimum 1° (1:60)
  - Fastener requirements:
    - Class 4 minimum for Colorbond
    - Class 3 for Zincalume (non-marine)
    - Class 4 minimum for marine environments
  - Flashing requirements:
    - 150mm minimum upstand at walls
    - 450mm minimum valley flashing width
    - 200mm minimum apron flashing cover

- **AS 1562.1:2018** - Design and Installation of Metal Wall and Sheet Cladding
- **AS 4046 Series** - Methods of Testing Roof Tiles
- **AS 1397:2021** - Steel Sheet and Strip
  - BMT requirements: 0.42mm residential, 0.48mm commercial
  - Coating mass: AM100 inland, AM150 coastal, AM200 severe marine

### NCC 2022 Requirements
- Part 7.2.2: Sheet roofing materials
- Part 7.2.3: Sheet roofing fixing
- Sarking requirements per AS/NZS 4200.1 and 4200.2

### Safety Requirements (SafeWork)
- Work above 2 metres requires fall prevention
- Hierarchy: eliminate → passive prevention → work positioning → fall arrest
- Scaffolds over 4m require licensed scaffolder
- Edge protection required where practicable

## 10. WORK HEALTH & SAFETY (ALL TRADES)

### Primary Legislation
- **WHS Act 2011** - National model WHS laws
  - General duty of care
  - Risk management hierarchy: eliminate → substitute → engineer → admin → PPE
  - Consultation requirements
  - Incident notification

### Safe Work Method Statements (SWMS)
- Required for all high-risk construction work
- Must identify hazards and control measures
- Regular review and updates
- Worker acknowledgment required

### Common Requirements
- PPE appropriate to task
- First aid provisions
- Emergency procedures
- Training and competency records
- Site inductions
