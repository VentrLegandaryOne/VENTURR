# Labor Pricing Knowledge Base for Venturr Dual-Intelligence System

## Overview

This knowledge base provides comprehensive information about Australian roofing labor pricing, specifically optimized for Colorbond metal roofing on the East Coast. The Dual-Intelligence system should use this information to provide accurate cost estimates, crew recommendations, and project planning advice.

## Skill Levels and Base Rates (2025)

### Apprentice
- **Base hourly rate**: $22.00 AUD
- **Experience**: Less than 1 year
- **Suitable for**: Assisting qualified tradespeople, basic tasks
- **Percentile**: 10th (entry-level)

### Junior Tradesperson
- **Base hourly rate**: $26.00 AUD
- **Experience**: 1-4 years
- **Suitable for**: Standard installation tasks under supervision
- **Percentile**: 25th (early career)

### Qualified Tradesperson
- **Base hourly rate**: $32.00 AUD
- **Experience**: 5-9 years
- **Suitable for**: Independent work, standard to complex installations
- **Percentile**: 50th (median, most common)

### Senior Tradesperson
- **Base hourly rate**: $38.00 AUD
- **Experience**: 10-19 years
- **Suitable for**: Complex installations, mentoring, quality control
- **Percentile**: 75th (experienced)

### Supervisor/Foreman
- **Base hourly rate**: $45.00 AUD
- **Experience**: 20+ years
- **Suitable for**: Crew management, complex projects, client liaison
- **Percentile**: 90th (highly experienced)

## Mandatory On-Costs (2025-26)

### Superannuation Guarantee
- **Rate**: 12.0% of Ordinary Time Earnings
- **Effective from**: 1 July 2025
- **Description**: Mandatory employer contribution to superannuation
- **Calculation**: Applied to base wage before other on-costs

### WorkCover Insurance (by state)

#### NSW
- **Rate**: 10.81% of wages
- **Breakdown**: 10.70% WorkCover + 0.11% Dust Disease Contribution
- **Industry**: Roofing Services (WIC Code 422300)
- **Note**: Highest rate on East Coast due to high-risk classification

#### Queensland
- **Rate**: 4.5% of wages (estimated for roofing)
- **Average rate**: 1.343% (roofing is 2.5-3.5x higher)
- **Industry**: Roofing Services
- **Note**: Held steady from 2024-25

#### Victoria
- **Rate**: 7.0% of wages (estimated for roofing)
- **Historical range**: 5.478% to 8.073%
- **Industry**: Roofing Services
- **Note**: Recent increases suggest upper end more likely

### Public Liability Insurance
- **Rate**: 2.5% of labor cost
- **Description**: Insurance for third-party injury or property damage
- **Mandatory**: Yes, for all commercial roofing work

### PPE & Safety Equipment
- **Rate**: 2.0% of labor cost
- **Description**: Personal protective equipment and safety gear
- **Mandatory**: Yes, for workplace safety compliance

## Optional On-Costs

### Tools & Equipment
- **Rate**: 3.0% of labor cost
- **Description**: Depreciation and maintenance of tools and equipment
- **Recommendation**: Include for accurate full-cost pricing

### Vehicle Costs
- **Rate**: 4.0% of labor cost
- **Description**: Vehicle depreciation, fuel, and maintenance
- **Recommendation**: Include for mobile crews

### Administration Overhead
- **Rate**: 5.0% of labor cost
- **Description**: Office costs, accounting, management
- **Recommendation**: Include for business sustainability

## Crew Compositions

### 1. Apprentice Duo (Budget Option)
- **Composition**: 1 Qualified Tradesperson + 1 Apprentice
- **Efficiency**: 80% of standard crew
- **Suitable for**: 
  - Simple residential jobs under 100m²
  - Budget-conscious clients
  - Straightforward installations
- **Hourly cost (Sydney, all on-costs)**: ~$95-105/hour
- **Best for**: Small, simple projects where cost is primary concern

### 2. Standard Crew (Most Common)
- **Composition**: 2 Qualified Tradespeople
- **Efficiency**: 100% (baseline)
- **Suitable for**:
  - Most residential jobs 100-200m²
  - Standard complexity roofs
  - Typical suburban installations
- **Hourly cost (Sydney, all on-costs)**: ~$140-155/hour
- **Best for**: Standard residential projects, most common choice

### 3. Enhanced Crew (Faster Completion)
- **Composition**: 1 Senior Tradesperson + 2 Qualified Tradespeople
- **Efficiency**: 140% of standard crew
- **Suitable for**:
  - Larger residential 200-300m²
  - Projects requiring faster completion
  - Moderate complexity
- **Hourly cost (Sydney, all on-costs)**: ~$215-235/hour
- **Best for**: Larger homes, tight deadlines, quality-focused clients

### 4. Premium Crew (Complex Projects)
- **Composition**: 1 Supervisor + 3 Qualified Tradespeople
- **Efficiency**: 180% of standard crew
- **Suitable for**:
  - Complex residential projects
  - Multi-level homes
  - Small commercial projects
  - High-end custom work
- **Hourly cost (Sydney, all on-costs)**: ~$280-310/hour
- **Best for**: Complex architecture, premium clients, challenging projects

### 5. Commercial Crew
- **Composition**: 1 Supervisor + 4 Qualified Tradespeople + 1 Apprentice
- **Efficiency**: 250% of standard crew
- **Suitable for**:
  - Large commercial projects
  - Industrial buildings
  - Projects over 400m²
- **Hourly cost (Sydney, all on-costs)**: ~$350-390/hour
- **Best for**: Commercial and industrial projects

## Installation Time Estimates

### Base Hours per Square Metre (by complexity)

#### Simple Roof
- **Base rate**: 0.30 hours/m²
- **Characteristics**: Single-level, rectangular, low pitch, minimal penetrations
- **Example**: Basic suburban home, gable roof

#### Standard Roof
- **Base rate**: 0.425 hours/m²
- **Characteristics**: Moderate complexity, some valleys, standard pitch
- **Example**: Typical residential home with 1-2 valleys

#### Complex Roof
- **Base rate**: 0.625 hours/m²
- **Characteristics**: Multi-level, steep pitch, multiple valleys, penetrations
- **Example**: Two-story home with complex architecture

#### Very Complex Roof
- **Base rate**: 1.0 hours/m²
- **Characteristics**: Custom design, very steep, difficult access, many features
- **Example**: Architectural home, heritage restoration, difficult site

### Pitch Multipliers

- **Low (0-15°)**: 1.0x (no adjustment)
- **Moderate (15-30°)**: 1.15x (+15%)
- **Steep (30-45°)**: 1.35x (+35%)
- **Very Steep (45°+)**: 1.6x (+60%)

### Complexity Multipliers (based on valleys/hips)

#### Simple Roof
- Simple rectangular: 1.0x
- 1-2 valleys: 1.05x
- 3-5 valleys: 1.1x
- 6+ valleys: 1.2x

#### Standard Roof
- Simple rectangular: 1.0x
- 1-2 valleys: 1.1x
- 3-5 valleys: 1.25x
- 6+ valleys: 1.5x

#### Complex Roof
- Simple rectangular: 1.0x
- 1-2 valleys: 1.15x
- 3-5 valleys: 1.35x
- 6+ valleys: 1.7x

#### Very Complex Roof
- Simple rectangular: 1.0x
- 1-2 valleys: 1.2x
- 3-5 valleys: 1.5x
- 6+ valleys: 2.0x

## Calculation Examples

### Example 1: Standard Sydney Residential (150m²)

**Project Details:**
- Roof area: 150m²
- Complexity: Standard
- Pitch: Moderate (22.5°)
- Valleys: 2
- Region: Sydney Metro
- Crew: Standard Crew (2 qualified tradespeople)

**Calculation:**
1. Base hours: 150m² × 0.425 hours/m² = 63.75 hours
2. Pitch multiplier: 63.75 × 1.15 = 73.31 hours
3. Complexity multiplier (1-2 valleys): 73.31 × 1.1 = 80.64 hours
4. Crew efficiency: 80.64 ÷ 1.0 = 80.64 hours (no adjustment for standard crew)
5. Days required: 80.64 ÷ 8 hours = 11 days (rounded up)

**Labor Cost:**
- Base hourly rate (2 tradespeople): $32 × 2 = $64/hour
- Regional adjustment (+15%): $64 × 1.15 = $73.60/hour
- Superannuation (12%): $73.60 × 0.12 = $8.83/hour
- WorkCover NSW (10.81%): $73.60 × 0.1081 = $7.96/hour
- Public Liability (2.5%): $73.60 × 0.025 = $1.84/hour
- PPE & Safety (2.0%): $73.60 × 0.02 = $1.47/hour
- **Total hourly rate**: $73.60 + $8.83 + $7.96 + $1.84 + $1.47 = $93.70/hour

**Total Labor Cost:**
- 80.64 hours × $93.70/hour = **$7,555.97**
- Cost per m²: $7,555.97 ÷ 150m² = **$50.37/m²**

### Example 2: Complex Brisbane Project (250m²)

**Project Details:**
- Roof area: 250m²
- Complexity: Complex
- Pitch: Steep (35°)
- Valleys: 5
- Region: Brisbane Metro
- Crew: Enhanced Crew (1 senior + 2 qualified)

**Calculation:**
1. Base hours: 250m² × 0.625 hours/m² = 156.25 hours
2. Pitch multiplier: 156.25 × 1.35 = 210.94 hours
3. Complexity multiplier (3-5 valleys): 210.94 × 1.35 = 284.77 hours
4. Crew efficiency: 284.77 ÷ 1.4 = 203.41 hours
5. Days required: 203.41 ÷ 8 hours = 26 days (rounded up)

**Labor Cost:**
- Base hourly rate: ($38 × 1) + ($32 × 2) = $102/hour
- Regional adjustment (+10%): $102 × 1.10 = $112.20/hour
- Superannuation (12%): $112.20 × 0.12 = $13.46/hour
- WorkCover QLD (4.5%): $112.20 × 0.045 = $5.05/hour
- Public Liability (2.5%): $112.20 × 0.025 = $2.81/hour
- PPE & Safety (2.0%): $112.20 × 0.02 = $2.24/hour
- **Total hourly rate**: $112.20 + $13.46 + $5.05 + $2.81 + $2.24 = $135.76/hour

**Total Labor Cost:**
- 203.41 hours × $135.76/hour = **$27,614.94**
- Cost per m²: $27,614.94 ÷ 250m² = **$110.46/m²**

### Common Questions and Answers

**Q: Why is labor so expensive in Sydney?**
A: Sydney has a 15% regional adjustment due to very high cost of living. Additionally, NSW has the highest WorkCover rate at 10.81% compared to 4.5% in QLD and 7% in VIC. These factors combined can add 20-25% to base labor costs.

**Q: How long will my roof take to install?**
A: For a standard 150m² residential roof with moderate complexity in Sydney, expect 10-11 days with a standard 2-person crew. Complex roofs or larger areas will take longer. We can expedite with a larger crew if needed.

**Q: What's included in the labor cost?**
A: Labor costs include the crew's wages plus all mandatory on-costs: superannuation (12%), WorkCover insurance (varies by state), public liability insurance (2.5%), and safety equipment (2%). Optional costs like tools, vehicles, and administration can be added for full-cost pricing.

**Q: Should I use a bigger crew to finish faster?**
A: It depends on your priorities. A larger crew costs more per hour but finishes faster, potentially saving on total project time. For a 200m² complex roof, an Enhanced Crew might cost 50% more per hour but finish in 60% of the time, potentially saving money overall.

**Q: How accurate are these estimates?**
A: These estimates are based on 2025 industry data from PayScale Australia, ATO superannuation rates, and state WorkCover authorities. Actual costs may vary by 10-15% based on specific contractor rates, project conditions, and market factors. Always get multiple quotes for large projects.
