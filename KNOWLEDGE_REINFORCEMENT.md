### 1.1 SA HB 39:2015 - Installation Code for Metal Roof and Wall Cladding

**Status:** Current Australian Standard Handbook (2015 edition, incorporating Amendment No. 1)

**Purpose:**
- Provides information and guidelines on selection, performance, and installation of metal roofing and wall cladding
- Serves as industry best practice guide for training providers and installers
- Complements Australian Standards AS 1562.1

**Key Coverage Areas:**
1. Material selection and compatibility
2. Corrosion protection requirements
3. Minimum roof pitches for different profiles
4. Installation procedures and techniques
5. Fastening requirements
6. Flashing details and weatherproofing
7. Safety requirements during installation

### 1.2 NCC 2022 - National Construction Code

**Status:** Current Australian Building Code (adopted May 2023, energy requirements effective October 2023)

**Relevant Sections for Roofing:**

**Section 7 - Roof and Wall Cladding (Housing Provisions):**
- Part 7.1: Scope and application
- Part 7.2: Sheet roofing
- Part 7.3: Roof tiles and shingles
- Part 7.4: Gutters and downpipes
- Part 7.5: Timber and composite wall cladding

**Part F3 - Roof and Wall Cladding (Volume One):**
- Weatherproofing requirements
- Structural requirements
- Minimizing risk of water entry
- Prevention of damp and unhealthy conditions

**Key Requirements:**
1. **Weatherproofing:** Prevent water entry from rain, surface water
2. **Structural:** Withstand wind loads per AS/NZS 1170.2
3. **Corrosion Protection:** Material compatibility and protection
4. **Minimum Pitches:** Specific to roofing profile type
5. **Energy Efficiency:** 7-star rating for building shell (roof, walls, windows, floors)
6. **Roof Ventilation:** Specific ventilation requirements based on roof pitch
7. **Thermal Breaks:** R-value 0.2 minimum for lightweight cladding

**Venturr System Integration:**
- ✅ NCC 2022 compliance explicitly stated
- ✅ Weatherproofing requirements addressed
- ✅ Structural requirements (wind loads) referenced
- ⚠️ Energy efficiency (7-star) not explicitly addressed

- ⚠️ Thermal break requirements not mentioned
