#### Clarity
- **Criterion**: Information is easy to understand
- **Indicators**:
  - ✅ No jargon or technical terms without explanation
  - ✅ Logical structure and flow
  - ✅ Key information highlighted
  - ✅ Visual hierarchy is clear
  - ✅ Language is simple and direct

#### Compliance
- **Criterion**: Outputs meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Indicators**:
  - ✅ Australian building codes referenced
  - ✅ Safety standards documented
  - ✅ Insurance requirements met
  - ✅ Warranty terms clear
  - ✅ Liability properly allocated

- **Criterion**: Outputs look professional and trustworthy

  - ✅ Professional photos and visuals

  - ✅ Risks are explicitly listed
  - ✅ Exclusions are clearly marked

  - ✅ Contingencies are identified

## PART 4: CONTINUOUS SIMULATION & VALIDATION LOOP

### Simulation Process

#### Step 1: Generate Output
```
1. System generates document/quote/report
2. Output includes all required sections
3. Data is accurate and complete
4. Formatting is professional
```

#### Step 2: Simulate Role-Based Perception
```
For each archetype:
1. Read the output as that archetype
2. Assess clarity and understanding
3. Check compliance requirements
4. Evaluate professionalism
5. Verify risk visibility
6. Score acceptance (1-10)
```

#### Step 3: Identify Gaps
```
1. Clarity gaps - Information hard to understand
2. Compliance gaps - Missing required information
3. Professionalism gaps - Poor formatting or presentation
4. Risk gaps - Risks not clearly visible
5. Completeness gaps - Missing sections or information
```

#### Step 4: Auto-Refine
```
1. Rewrite unclear sections
2. Add missing compliance information
3. Improve formatting and design
4. Highlight risks and exclusions
5. Add missing sections
```

#### Step 5: Re-Integrate
```
1. Update system with refined version
2. Test integration with other modules
3. Verify no breaking changes
4. Update related documents
```

#### Step 6: Re-Evaluate
```
1. Re-simulate with all archetypes
2. Score acceptance again
3. Verify improvement
4. Document changes
```

### Validation Scoring

#### Clarity Score (0-10)
- 9-10: Immediately understandable to all archetypes
- 7-8: Clear with minor clarifications needed
- 5-6: Understandable but requires effort
- 3-4: Confusing, needs significant revision
- 0-2: Incomprehensible, major revision required

#### Compliance Score (0-10)
- 9-10: Exceeds all requirements
- 7-8: Meets all requirements
- 5-6: Meets most requirements, minor gaps
- 3-4: Missing significant requirements
- 0-2: Major compliance gaps

#### Professionalism Score (0-10)
- 9-10: Exceeds professional standards
- 7-8: Professional and polished
- 5-6: Acceptable but could be better
- 3-4: Below professional standards
- 0-2: Unprofessional

#### Risk Visibility Score (0-10)
- 9-10: All risks clearly visible and explained
- 7-8: Most risks visible, minor gaps
- 5-6: Some risks visible, significant gaps
- 3-4: Few risks visible
- 0-2: Risks hidden or unclear

#### Overall Acceptance Score
```
Acceptance = (Clarity + Compliance + Professionalism + Risk Visibility) / 4
```

**Acceptance Thresholds**:
- 9-10: Ready for deployment
- 7-8: Minor refinements needed
- 5-6: Significant refinements needed
- 3-4: Major revision required
- 0-2: Reject and redesign
