### B. Optimal Process Framework

**NSW Roofing Project Workflow:**
1. **Initial Inquiry** (Day 0)
   - Capture: Address, roof type, urgency, budget expectations
   - Validate: Google Maps satellite view, street view analysis
   - Classify: Wind zone, coastal exposure, bushfire zone

2. **Site Assessment** (Day 1-3)
   - Measure: Roof area, pitch, complexity factors
   - Inspect: Existing structure, access, safety requirements
   - Document: Photos, measurements, client preferences

3. **Quote Generation** (Day 3-5)
   - Calculate: Materials (with supplier pricing), labor (NSW rates), compliance costs
   - Include: Itemized breakdown, warranty terms, timeline
   - Present: Professional PDF with company credentials, testimonials

4. **Client Acceptance** (Day 5-10)
   - Clarify: Scope, exclusions, payment terms
   - Secure: Deposit (30-50%), signed contract
   - Schedule: Start date, milestone dates

5. **Pre-Construction** (Day 10-15)
   - Order: Materials (lead time 5-10 days)
   - Arrange: Permits, insurance, site access
   - Confirm: Crew availability, equipment

6. **Installation** (Day 15-20)
   - Execute: Per HB 39, AS 1562.1, manufacturer specs
   - Inspect: Daily progress, quality checkpoints
   - Communicate: Client updates, photo documentation

7. **Completion** (Day 20-22)
   - Final Inspection: Compliance checklist, client walkthrough
   - Documentation: Warranty certificate, maintenance guide, compliance certificate
   - Payment: Final invoice, collect balance

8. **Post-Project** (Day 22+)
   - Follow-up: 7-day check-in, 30-day satisfaction survey
   - Warranty: Register with manufacturer, provide client copy
   - Referral: Request Google review, testimonial
