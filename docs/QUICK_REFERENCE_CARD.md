## Quick Calculation Formula

**Total Labor Hours = (Base Time × Area × Material Multiplier × Pitch Multiplier × Valley Multiplier + Removal Time) ÷ Crew Efficiency**

**Total Days = (Total Hours ÷ 8) × (1 + Weather Buffer)**

**Labor Cost = Total Hours × Crew Hourly Rate (including on-costs)**

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Underestimating Complexity** - Count valleys carefully, don't assume "simple"
2. **Forgetting Removal Time** - Re-roofing takes 30-50% longer than new construction
3. **Ignoring Weather** - Winter projects need 25% more time
4. **Wrong Crew Size** - Match crew to project scope and timeline
5. **Missing On-Costs** - Always include mandatory on-costs (super, WorkCover, insurance)
6. **Regional Adjustments** - Sydney costs 15% more than regional NSW
7. **Material Multipliers** - Tile takes 2-3x longer than metal

**Is it a re-roof?**
- Yes → Use Re-Roofing Specialist crew, add removal time
- No → Continue

**Is it emergency work?**
- Yes → Use Emergency Response crew, add premiums
- No → Continue

- No → Continue
