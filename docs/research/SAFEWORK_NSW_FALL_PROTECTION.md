## Key Legal Requirements

### Primary Duty
PCBUs (Persons Conducting a Business or Undertaking) must protect workers from the risk of falling from one level to another - **regardless of height**. On-the-spot fines apply for non-compliance.

### SWMS Requirement (Critical for Roofing)
**A Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS) is required for construction work where a person could fall more than 2 metres.**

Requirements for SWMS:
- Must be site-specific
- Must be available to workers, supervisors, and other persons at workplace
- Must detail hazards, risks, and safety controls

## Hierarchy of Control (Mandatory Order)

### 1. Work on Ground or Solid Construction (Preferred)
- If work at heights can be avoided, it must be
- Solid construction requirements:
  - Structurally sound with flat, stable surface
  - Capable of supporting weight of people and materials
  - Fall protection around perimeter and fall hazards
  - Safe means of entry and exit

### 2. Fall Prevention Devices (First Choice if Heights Required)
Examples include:
- Scaffolds
- Elevating work platforms
- Mast climbing work platforms
- Work boxes
- Building maintenance units
- Perimeter guardrails
- Secure fences/barriers/covers

### 3. Work Positioning Systems (Second Choice)
Only when fall-prevention device not reasonably practicable:
- Total restraint systems
- Industrial rope access systems

### 4. Fall Arrest Systems (Third Choice)
Only when neither fall-prevention nor work-positioning practicable:
- Industrial safety nets
- Catch platforms
- Harness-based fall-arrest systems

**Note:** Emergency and rescue procedures must be in place and tested.

### 5. Administrative Controls (Supplementary)
- Safe work procedures
- Safe sequencing of work
- Safe use of ladders
- Permit systems
- "No go" areas
- Signage

## Verification Implications for VALIDT

### Must Check in Roofing Quotes:
1. **SWMS Reference** - Quote should reference site-specific SWMS for work >2m
2. **Fall Protection Method** - Must specify which control measures will be used
3. **Hierarchy Compliance** - Higher-order controls should be prioritized
4. **Equipment Specifications** - Scaffolds, harnesses, edge protection details
5. **Worker Training** - Reference to competency requirements
6. **Emergency Procedures** - For fall arrest systems

### Red Flags to Identify:
- No mention of fall protection for roof work
- Ladder-only access for extended roof work
- No SWMS reference for work >2m height
- Fall arrest without emergency procedures
