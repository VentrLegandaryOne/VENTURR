## Key Standards for Metal Roofing

### AS 1397:2021 - Continuous Hot-Dip Metallic Coated Steel Sheet and Strip
**Citation:** AS 1397:2021, Standards Australia

**Scope:**
- Specifies requirements for continuously hot-dip metallic coated sheet steel and strip
- Applies to thicknesses up to and including 5.0mm
- Covers coatings of zinc and zinc alloyed with aluminium and magnesium

**Key Requirements:**
- Material thickness specifications
- Coating type requirements (zinc, aluminium/zinc alloy)
- Quality and testing requirements

**Status:** Current (superseded AS 1397-2011)

**URL:** https://www.standards.org.au/standards-catalogue/standard-details?designation=as-1397-2011

---

### AS 1562.1:2018 - Design and Installation of Metal Roof and Wall Cladding, Part 1: Metal
**Citation:** AS 1562.1:2018, Standards Australia

**Scope:**
- Provides designers and installers with minimum design requirements
- Covers correct and safe design and installation of sheet metal roof and wall cladding
- Applies to all classes of self-supporting sheet metal roof and wall cladding
- Covers both cyclone and non-cyclone regions

**Key Requirements:**
- Wind load calculations
- Fixing specifications
- Weatherproofing requirements
- Structural adequacy

**Referenced by:** NCC 2022 for demonstrating weatherproofing compliance

---

### SA HB 39:2015 - Installation Code for Metal Roof and Wall Cladding
**Citation:** SA HB 39:2015, Standards Australia

**Scope:**
- Companion document to AS 1562.1:2018
- Provides information and guidelines on selection, performance and installation
- Ensures weatherproof exterior and proper stormwater drainage

**Ten Sections:**

| Section | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| 1 | Scope, purpose, reference documents, definitions |
| 2 | Material thickness, compatibility, durability, sealants |
| 3 | Maintenance, site storage, temporary walkways, sheet protection |
| 4 | Roof safety, OH&S requirements |
| 5 | Gutters, downpipes, sumps, rainheads |
| 6 | Insulation methods, condensation control |
| 7 | Roof profiles, wind forces, pitch, lapping, curving |
| 8 | Roof flashing and capping |
| 9 | Translucent roof sheeting |
| 10 | Ceiling components and roof extras |

**Appendices:**
- Appendix A: Corrosion rates across seven atmospheric classifications
- Appendix B: Roof and wall insulated panels

**Note:** Not directly referenced in NCC but may be mandatory in State/Territory legislation.

**URL:** https://hia.com.au/resources-and-advice/building-it-right/australian-standards/articles/installation-code-for-metal-roof-and-wall-cladding

## Verification Implications for VALIDT

### Must Check in Roofing Quotes:

1. **Material Compliance (AS 1397)**
   - Steel sheet thickness specified
   - Coating type (e.g., Zincalume, Colorbond)
   - Coating mass/thickness grade

2. **Design Compliance (AS 1562.1)**
   - Wind region/classification stated
   - Fixing pattern specified
   - Span tables referenced

3. **Installation Compliance (HB 39)**
   - Safety measures for roof work
   - Flashing specifications
   - Gutter/downpipe sizing
   - Insulation R-value if applicable
