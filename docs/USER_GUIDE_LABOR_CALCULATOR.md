## Introduction

The Venturr Enhanced Labor Calculator is the most comprehensive roofing estimation tool in Australia. It provides accurate labor cost estimates by considering material types, crew composition, regional variations, weather delays, and removal requirements.

### What Makes It Different?

Unlike basic calculators that use simple square meter rates, the Enhanced Labor Calculator considers:

- **Material-specific installation times** - Tile takes 2.5x longer than metal
- **Crew efficiency variations** - Different crew sizes have different productivity levels
- **Regional cost adjustments** - Sydney costs 15% more than regional NSW
- **Weather delay buffers** - Winter projects need 25% more time
- **Removal complexity** - Old roof removal adds significant time
- **Mandatory on-costs** - Superannuation, WorkCover, insurance automatically calculated

### Who Should Use This?

- Roofing contractors preparing quotes
- Estimators calculating project costs
- Project managers planning schedules
- Business owners analyzing profitability

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Calculator

1. Log in to your Venturr account
2. Navigate to a project or create a new one
3. Click **Labor Calculator** in the Quick Actions section

### The Three-Tab Workflow

The calculator uses a logical three-step workflow:

1. **Dimensions** - Enter roof measurements

## Dimensions Tab - Roof Measurements

### Basic Measurements

**Roof Length (m)**
- Measure the longest dimension of the roof
- For complex roofs, break into sections and add together
- Example: 15.0m

**Roof Width (m)**
- Measure the width perpendicular to the length
- For irregular shapes, use the average width
- Example: 10.0m

**Roof Type**
- **Gable:** Simple two-sided roof (most common)
- **Hip:** Four-sided roof with slopes on all sides
- **Flat:** Low-pitch or flat roof
- **Complex:** Multiple levels, valleys, or irregular shapes

**Roof Pitch (degrees)**
- Measure the angle of the roof slope
- Common pitches:
  - Low: 5-15° (flat to gentle slope)
  - Moderate: 15-30° (standard residential)
  - Steep: 30-45° (steep residential)
  - Very Steep: 45°+ (architectural or heritage)

**Roofing Material**
- Select from the dropdown list
- Material choice affects labor time significantly
- Popular options:
  - TRIMDEK® 0.42 BMT ZINCALUME® - $28.50/m²
  - TRIMDEK® 0.42 BMT COLORBOND® - $35.00/m²
  - KLIP-LOK® 0.42 BMT COLORBOND® - $42.00/m²

**Waste Percentage (%)**
- Standard: 10% for simple roofs
- Complex: 15-20% for roofs with many cuts
- Accounts for offcuts, damaged sheets, and errors

### Calculated Results

After entering dimensions, the calculator automatically shows:

- **Roof Area:** Length × Width (m²)
- **Total Area (with waste):** Roof Area × (1 + Waste%)
- **Sheets Required:** Based on material coverage
- **Fasteners:** Calculated per sheet requirements

## Labor Tab - Crew and Project Configuration

This is where the Enhanced Labor Calculator truly shines. You configure all the factors that affect labor costs.

### Roof Complexity

**Simple (Basic rectangular roof)**
- Single plane, minimal cuts
- No valleys or complex features
- Base time: 0.30 hrs/m²
- Example: Basic gable shed

**Standard (Moderate complexity)**
- Standard residential roof
- 1-2 valleys or hips
- Base time: 0.425 hrs/m²
- Example: Typical suburban home

**Complex (Multiple features)**
- Multiple valleys and hips
- Different roof levels
- Base time: 0.625 hrs/m²
- Example: Multi-level home

**Very Complex (Highly intricate)**
- Many valleys, hips, and penetrations
- Custom architectural features
- Base time: 0.875 hrs/m²
- Example: Architect-designed home

### Pitch Category

The system automatically suggests pitch category based on your entered angle:

- **Low (0-15°):** 1.0x multiplier
- **Moderate (15-30°):** 1.15x multiplier
- **Steep (30-45°):** 1.35x multiplier
- **Very Steep (45°+):** 1.6x multiplier

Steeper roofs require more time for safety and difficulty.

### Number of Valleys/Hips

Enter the count of valleys and hips on the roof:

- **0:** Simple rectangular roof (1.0x)
- **1-2:** Few valleys (1.05-1.1x)
- **3-5:** Moderate valleys (1.1-1.25x)
- **6+:** Many valleys (1.2-1.5x)

Each valley adds complexity and installation time.

**Standard Crew** ⭐ Most Common

- Hourly rate: Standard market rate

**Enhanced Crew (Faster)**

- Best for: Larger residential 200-300m²
- Hourly rate: Premium but faster completion

**Premium Crew (Complex)**

- Hourly rate: Premium for complex work

**Commercial Crew**
- 1 Supervisor + 4 Qualified + 1 Apprentice

- Hourly rate: Commercial rates

**Material Type (Labor Impact)**

**Existing Roof Removal**

If re-roofing, select what needs to be removed:

- **None (New construction):** No removal time
- **Metal Roof:** +0.15 hrs/m² - Quick removal
- **Terracotta Tile:** +0.25 hrs/m² - Moderate removal time
- **Concrete Tile:** +0.30 hrs/m² - Heavy, slow removal

## Understanding Your Results

After clicking **Calculate Takeoff**, you'll see comprehensive results:

### Roof Details Section

- **Roof Area:** Base measurement in m²
- **Total Area (with waste):** Includes waste percentage
- **Sheets Required:** Number of roofing sheets needed
- **Fasteners:** Total fastener count

### Labor Details Section

This is the heart of the calculator:

**Crew:** Shows selected crew composition

**Region:** Your selected location with adjustment

**Installation Hours:** Time for actual installation

**Total Hours:** Installation + removal (if applicable)

**Base Days Required:** Total hours ÷ 8 hours per day

**Weather Delay Buffer:** Extra days for weather (e.g., +1 days for summer)

**Total Days (with weather):** Realistic project duration

**Crew Rate (inc. on-costs):** Hourly rate including all costs

**Labor Cost per m²:** Cost per square meter

### Cost Breakdown Section

**Materials:** Total material cost from selected products

**Labor:** Total labor cost (hours × crew rate)

**Total (with profit):** Subtotal with profit margin applied

**GST (10%):** Goods and Services Tax

### Grand Total

The final project cost including everything.

**Per m²:** Cost per square meter for easy comparison

### Crew Breakdown Section

Detailed transparency of labor costs:

**Crew Composition**
- Shows each role and hourly rate
- Example: "2x Qualified Tradesperson: $36.80/hr = $73.60/hr total"

**On-costs Included**
- Superannuation Guarantee: 12% (mandatory)
- WorkCover NSW: 10.81% (mandatory, varies by state)
- Public Liability Insurance: 2.5% (mandatory)
- Tools & Equipment: 3% (optional)
- PPE & Safety Equipment: 2% (mandatory)
- Vehicle Costs: 4% (optional)
- Administration Overhead: 5% (optional)

**Total Crew Rate:** Sum of all wages and on-costs

This breakdown shows clients exactly where their money goes and justifies your pricing.

## Tips for Accurate Estimates

### 1. Measure Carefully

- Use accurate measurements from site visits or plans
- Break complex roofs into simple sections
- Add sections together for total area
- Account for overhangs and eaves

### 2. Choose the Right Complexity

- Don't underestimate complexity
- Count valleys and hips carefully
- Consider access difficulties
- Factor in penetrations (skylights, vents)

### 3. Select Appropriate Crew

- Match crew size to project scope
- Consider project timeline requirements
- Balance cost vs. speed
- Use specialized crews for specialized work

### 4. Account for Removal

- Always include removal time for re-roofing
- Asbestos requires licensed removal
- Disposal costs are separate from labor
- Removal can take 30-50% of total time

### 5. Be Realistic About Weather

- Winter projects need bigger buffers
- Coastal areas have more weather delays
- Build in contingency time
- Communicate realistic timelines to clients

### 6. Include All On-Costs

- Always include mandatory on-costs
- Optional on-costs improve accuracy
- Show clients the full breakdown
- Justify your pricing with transparency

### 7. Regional Adjustments Matter

- Sydney and Gold Coast have significant premiums
- Regional areas may be baseline or lower
- Consider travel time for remote locations
- Adjust for local market conditions

### 8. Material Selection Impacts Labor

- Tile takes 2-3x longer than metal
- Custom materials require specialist crews
- Consider material availability
- Factor in lead times for special orders

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Results Seem Too High

**Possible Causes:**
- Complexity set too high
- Wrong crew selected (too large)
- Optional on-costs included unnecessarily
- Material type multiplier too high

**Solutions:**
- Review complexity setting
- Try a smaller crew if appropriate
- Uncheck optional on-costs if not needed
- Verify material type selection

### Issue: Results Seem Too Low

**Possible Causes:**
- Complexity set too low
- Crew too small for project
- Removal time not included
- Weather delays not considered

**Solutions:**
- Increase complexity if roof is intricate
- Select larger crew for faster completion
- Add removal type if re-roofing
- Choose appropriate season

### Issue: Days Required Seems Unrealistic

**Possible Causes:**
- Crew efficiency not considered
- Weather buffer too high/low
- Complexity mismatch

**Solutions:**
- Review crew selection
- Adjust season if necessary
- Verify complexity setting matches actual roof

### Issue: Labor Cost per m² Very Different from Market

**Possible Causes:**
- Regional adjustment incorrect
- Crew composition inappropriate
- Material type wrong

**Solutions:**
- Verify region selection
- Compare with similar projects
- Check material type multiplier
- Review on-costs inclusion
