### Enhanced Labor Calculator

**How to Access:**
1. Create or open a project
2. Click "Labor Calculator" in the Quick Actions section

**What You'll See:**
Three-tab interface with comprehensive labor pricing:

#### Tab 1: Dimensions
- **Roof Length** - Enter in meters (e.g., 12.0)
- **Roof Width** - Enter in meters (e.g., 9.0)
- **Roof Type** - Select: Gable, Hip, Skillion, or Flat
- **Roof Pitch** - Enter in degrees (e.g., 22.5)
- **Roofing Material** - Choose from Colorbond options
- **Waste Percentage** - Default 10%, adjust as needed

#### Tab 2: Labor Configuration
- **Roof Complexity** - Simple, Standard, Complex, Very Complex
- **Pitch Category** - Auto-calculated from pitch entered
- **Number of Valleys/Hips** - Enter count for complexity
- **Crew Composition** - 5 options from Apprentice Duo to Commercial Crew
- **Region** - Sydney, Brisbane, Melbourne, Newcastle, Gold Coast
- **Optional On-Costs** - Toggle for tools, vehicles, admin

**Advanced Factors (Scroll Down):**
- **Material Type** - Affects labor time (Colorbond 1.0x to Slate 3.0x)
- **Existing Roof Removal** - None to Slate removal options
- **Project Season** - Summer to Winter (affects weather delays)

#### Tab 3: Pricing
- **Profit Margin** - Set your desired margin percentage
- **Include GST** - Toggle GST calculation

**Try This Complete Workflow:**

1. **Enter Dimensions:**
   - Length: 15m
   - Width: 10m
   - Type: Gable
   - Pitch: 22.5°
   - Material: TRIMDEK® 0.42 BMT ZINCALUME®
   - Waste: 10%

   - Complexity: Standard

   - Region: Sydney Metro, NSW
   - Enable Optional On-Costs

3. **Set Advanced Factors:**
   - Material Type: Colorbond/Metal (Fastest)

4. **Set Pricing:**

### Regional Adjustments

**The calculator automatically applies regional cost-of-living adjustments:**

- **Sydney Metro, NSW:** +15% (highest)
- **Brisbane, QLD:** +10%
- **Melbourne, VIC:** +12%
- **Newcastle, NSW:** +8%
- **Gold Coast, QLD:** +10%

**State-Specific WorkCover Rates:**
- **NSW:** 10.81% (highest, due to roofing risk classification)
- **QLD:** 4.5%
- **VIC:** 7.0%

**Try This:**
Create the same project in different regions and compare the labor costs. You'll see significant differences based on location.

### Material Labor Multipliers

**Test How Different Materials Affect Labor Time:**

3. **Concrete Tile:** 2.5x (2.5x the time)

### Weather Delay Factors

**Seasonal Impact on Project Duration:**

1. **Summer (Dec-Feb):** +7.5% buffer (best weather)
2. **Autumn (Mar-May):** +12.5% buffer
3. **Winter (Jun-Aug):** +25% buffer (worst weather, highest delays)
4. **Spring (Sep-Nov):** +17.5% buffer

**Try This:**
1. Calculate a project in Summer
2. Note the total days required
3. Change season to Winter
4. See how many extra days are added for weather delays
